## 🚀 How to Use

### 1. Download Theme Package
File: `/app/bleame-shopify-theme.zip`

### 2. Upload to Shopify
1. Go to Shopify Admin → Online Store → Themes
2. Click "Add theme" → "Upload zip file"
3. Select `bleame-shopify-theme.zip`
4. Click "Customize" to edit
5. Click "Publish" when ready

### 3. Configure Your Store
Follow the detailed instructions in:
- `/app/SHOPIFY_SETUP_GUIDE.md`
- `/app/shopify-theme/README.md`

## 💡 Pro Tips for Success

1. **Start with Products:** Add at least 4-6 products before customizing
2. **Use Real Images:** High-quality product photos (800x800px minimum)
3. **Set Prices Wisely:** Use "compare at price" to show discounts
4. **Mobile First:** Test everything on mobile devices
5. **Social Proof:** Add real customer testimonials
6. **Clear CTAs:** Make buttons obvious and action-oriented
7. **Fast Loading:** Compress images before uploading
8. **Test Cart:** Complete a full test purchase
